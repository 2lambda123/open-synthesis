Welcome to the Open Synthesis Project!

Open Synthesis is an open platform for intelligence analysis. We're taking the best practices from the intelligence and business communities and adapting them to work at internet-scale.

Every day, the world's population creates 2.5 quintillion bytes of data. Our goal is to empower the public distill this information into actionable analysis.

Want to help? Become an analyst today!

What is the Analysis of Competing Hypotheses (ACH)?

Created at the CIA, ACH is a simple tool for analyzing complex situations. To perform ACH for an analysis question, analysts first brainstorm a set of hypotheses that could answer the question. Analysts then compile a list of all of relevant evidence (information). For each piece of evidence, each analyst determines which hypotheses are consistent with that evidence. Once all the evidence and hypotheses have been evaluated, that analysts determine which hypotheses are most likely given the evidence.
